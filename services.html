Ancient Celtic Divination

Ogham Reading

The Ogham is one of the oldest divination systems in the Western world — an ancient Irish alphabet carved into standing stones, each letter representing a sacred tree and the wisdom it carries. When cast or drawn, the Ogham staves speak directly to the forces of nature moving through your life.

Unlike linear card readings, Ogham works in patterns and relationships, revealing the web of influence surrounding your question. It is particularly potent for questions of ancestral lineage, spiritual path, and deep-rooted life patterns.

  • Ogham stave casting and interpretation
  • Tree symbolism and nature spirit guidance
  • Ancestral and lineage insight
  • Seasonal and cyclical timing guidance

The Living Tradition

Leson Voudou

Vodou is not what popular culture has made it. It is a living, breathing spiritual tradition rooted in West African cosmology, carried through the African diaspora, and refined in the spiritual crucible of Haiti. A Leson Voudou — a Vodou lesson — is a sacred educational session with an initiated Vodouizan.

Whether you are drawn to the lwa (spirits) for the first time, seeking to understand your own spiritual lineage, or deepening an existing practice, these sessions offer grounded, respectful, firsthand knowledge of the tradition as it is lived — not as it is mythologized.

  • Introduction to the lwa and the Vodou cosmology
  • Working with your personal spiritual court
  • Understanding altars, offerings, and ceremonies
  • Spiritual lineage and ancestry work
  • Ethical practice and cultural context
  • Custom learning path for ongoing students
